Creamy Mushroom Chicken Recipe

Recipe description

Mushroom chicken is a delicious and comforting dish that combines tender chicken with a rich, savory mushroom sauce. The dish is often prepared by sautéing chicken breasts or thighs with mushrooms, onions, and garlic, then finishing with a creamy or wine-based sauce. It's a versatile recipe that can be served with rice, pasta, or crusty bread, making it a favorite in many households for its ease of preparation and satisfying flavor.

    Ingredients

    For the Chicken
  • 4 boneless, skinless chicken breasts (or thighs)
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • ½ teaspoon black pepper
  • 1 teaspoon paprika (optional)
  • 2 tablespoons cooking oil or butter

    • For the Mushroom Sauce

  • 2 cups mushrooms, sliced
  • 1 small onion, finely chopped
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 cup chicken stock
  • ¾ cup cooking cream (or heavy cream)
  • 1 teaspoon dried thyme or mixed herbs
  • 1 tablespoon flour (optional, for thickening)
  • 2 tablespoons butter
  • Fresh parsley for garnish (optional)

Preparation Steps

  1. Season the Chicken

    2. Season chicken with salt, pepper, and paprika on both sides.

  2. Sear the Chicken

    3. Heat oil or butter in a large pan over medium heat. Cook the chicken for 5–6 minutes per side until golden brown. Remove from the pan and set aside.

  3. Cook the Mushrooms

    4. In the same pan, add butter and sauté mushrooms until they release moisture and become lightly browned.

  4. Add Aromatics

    5. Add chopped onion and cook until soft. Stir in garlic and cook for about 1 minute until fragrant.

  5. Prepare the Sauce

    6. Sprinkle flour (if using) and stir well. Gradually add chicken stock while stirring to avoid lumps. Allow the sauce to simmer for 2–3 minutes.

  6. Add Cream and Herbs

    7. Pour in the cream and add thyme or mixed herbs. Stir and let the sauce gently simmer until slightly thickened.

  7. Return the Chicken

    8. Place the chicken back into the pan, spoon sauce over it, and simmer for 8–10 minutes until the chicken is fully cooked and tender.

  8. Garnish and Serve

    9. Sprinkle fresh parsley on top and serve warm with rice, pasta, or vegetables.
